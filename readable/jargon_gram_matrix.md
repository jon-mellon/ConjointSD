# Gram matrix

Lean entrypoint: [ConjointSD.lean](../ConjointSD.lean)

The Gram matrix is the matrix of inner products of features, e.g., `X'X/n` in linear regression. It summarizes feature correlations and determines whether the normal equations are solvable.
