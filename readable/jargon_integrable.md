# Integrable

Lean entrypoint: [ConjointSD.lean](../ConjointSD.lean)

A function is integrable if its [average](jargon_mean.md) ([integral](jargon_integral.md)) exists and is finite under the given [distribution](jargon_distribution.md). This is a technical condition that lets you safely take expectations.
