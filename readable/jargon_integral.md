# Integral

Lean entrypoint: [ConjointSD.lean](../ConjointSD.lean)

An integral is a weighted [average](jargon_mean.md) of a function under a [distribution](jargon_distribution.md). In this project, integrals are used to define [population](jargon_population.md) averages like the [mean](jargon_mean.md), [second moment](jargon_second_moment.md), [variance](jargon_variance.md), and [standard deviation](jargon_standard_deviation.md).
