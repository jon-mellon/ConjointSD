# Linear-in-terms

Lean entrypoint: [ConjointSD.lean](../ConjointSD.lean)

A linear-in-terms model expresses a score as a sum over [terms](jargon_term.md): coefficient(term) times feature(term, x). It is linear in the coefficients, not necessarily linear in x itself.
