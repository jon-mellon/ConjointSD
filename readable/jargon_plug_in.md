# Plug-in

Lean entrypoint: [ConjointSD.lean](../ConjointSD.lean)

A plug-in estimate takes an [estimator](jargon_estimator.md) of a [parameter](jargon_parameter.md) and plugs it into a formula that normally uses the true parameter. It is the standard way to turn a [population](jargon_population.md) formula into an estimator.
