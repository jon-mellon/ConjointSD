# Second moment

Lean entrypoint: [ConjointSD.lean](../ConjointSD.lean)

The second moment of a quantity is the [average](jargon_mean.md) of its square. It is used together with the [mean](jargon_mean.md) to compute [variance](jargon_variance.md).
